Splinter Cell Blacklist FUI by Ubisoft Toronto

Posted by saji8k on August 20, 2013

Splinter Cell: Blacklist is the latest installment to Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell series was released today for the Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, Wii U and PC. The game received positive reviews with Metacritic scores of 86 and 82 for the Playstation 3 and Xbox 360 versions respectively. The FUI centerpiece of the game is the Strategic Mission Interface (SMI). Located in the "Paladin", the mobile headquarters of Forth Echelon, the SMI is a giant map where players begin single, co-op or multiplayer missions. Splinter Cell: Blacklist is the first game to come from Ubisoft Toronto.

District 9 FUI by Goldtooth Creative

Posted by saji8k on August 08, 2013

With Neill Blomkamp's second feature film, Elysium, coming out this Friday, it seems like a great time to revisit the FUI in his first film, District 9. The movie featured holographic alien user interfaces in several scenes, the longest of which was when Christopher Johnson was starting up the drop ship. A seven man team at Goldtooth Creative designed and animated the FUIs over a period of two months using Adobe After Effects and The Foundry's Nuke. The final composites were made by VFX company Image Engine and the end result can be seen in the video below.

Ender's Game Trailer 2 FUI

Posted by saji8k on August 06, 2013

The second trailer for Ender's Game was released today. It features several new FUI shots as well as a few shots seen in the previous trailer. The movie features FUI work by Ash Thorp, who was the Art Director and designer for the UI and HUD for the film, as well as Jayse Hansen and Navarro Parker. Ender's Game, which stars Asa Butterfield and Harrison Ford, will arrive in theatres in North America on November 1st.
