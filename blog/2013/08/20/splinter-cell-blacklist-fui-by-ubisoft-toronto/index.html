Splinter Cell Blacklist FUI by Ubisoft Toronto

Posted by saji8k on August 20, 2013

Splinter Cell: Blacklist is the latest installment to Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell series was released today for the Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, Wii U and PC. The game received positive reviews with Metacritic scores of 86 and 82 for the Playstation 3 and Xbox 360 versions respectively. The FUI centerpiece of the game is the Strategic Mission Interface (SMI). Located in the "Paladin", the mobile headquarters of Forth Echelon, the SMI is a giant map where players begin single, co-op or multiplayer missions. Splinter Cell: Blacklist is the first game to come from Ubisoft Toronto.
