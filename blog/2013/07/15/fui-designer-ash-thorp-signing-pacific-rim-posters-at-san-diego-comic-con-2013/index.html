FUI Designer Ash Thorp signing Pacific Rim Posters at San Diego Comic Con 2013

Posted by saji8k on July 15, 2013

Ash Thorp, whose FUI design credits include Total Recall and Prometheus, will be signing some limited edition Pacific Rim posters at San Diego Comic Con 2013. There is a Kaiju and Jager poster which look well together or on their own. The posters will be on sale at Comic Con on preview night July 17 for $50 each. There are only 350 of each available so get there early. Ash will be signing the posters at 5PM on July 18 at the Mondo booth (#936) so drop by and ask him how the FUI for Enders Game is going. A third unannounced poster by Ash will also be available.
